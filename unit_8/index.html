Who I Am

My professional experience in LA has taken me around the production world and back again — accumulating experience to make me one of the most versatile, well-rounded producers you’ll likely meet. Most recently I was an Interactive Producer of websites and social media content for such high-profile clients as Warner Brothers Interactive Entertainment, 20th Century Fox, Reebok and E&J Gallo Wines among many others. Prior to that, my experience included:

  1. - Line producer for an independent feature film that shot in Bogota, Colombia.
  2. - Producer of custom digital ad products for prominent clients that ran across the Viacom network.
  3. - Production manager for a 3D film shoot for a Resident Evil 5 commercial.
  4. - Producer for a motion graphics commercial for Honda
  5. - Production Manager for the live action elements of the Jimmy Kimmel show opening sequence.
  6. - I even coordinated on multiple seasons of the reality show Big Brother.

But, of course, it’s not all business. In my personal time I’m an avid cyclist, skier, craft beer bottle hunter (and amature homebrewer) and all-round Twin Peaks fanatic.
